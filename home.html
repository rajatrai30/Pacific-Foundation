DISASTER PREPAREDNESS

Pacific Foundation considers disaster preparedness and response as a critical aspect of its organisational mandate and has a ‘Disaster Preparedness and Response Strategy’ in place. Pacific Foundation also maintains Emergency Response Teams at National and State levels in the country to be able to make timely decisions.

Overview

Pacific Foundation is a Foundation for Social Change and Inclusion founded in this year of 2017 and registered in the Register of Non- Government Organizations in Mumbai, Maharashtra as well as the Central Register of Non- Government Organizations at the Ministry of Social Justice. The projects of Charitable Educational Institutes, Hospitals range from one-off humanitarian actions in response to a specific urgent need to long- term residential care and delivery of basic services in marginalized communities. They aim to help the most vulnerable members of our society and include work on different levels.

  1. Analysis and assessment of needs;
  2. Developing programmes and strategies for development;
  3. Empowering and developing local communities;
  4. Interaction with stakeholders;
